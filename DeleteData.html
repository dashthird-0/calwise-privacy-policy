CalWise - Account Deletion Request

This page explains how to request deletion of your CalWise account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the CalWise app on your device
  2. Navigate to the Profile tab
  3. Tap on "Sign Out" (This ensures your account session is ended)
  4. Send an email to calwise.dev@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  5. In your email, please include:
    • The email address associated with your CalWise account
    • A statement that you wish to delete your account and all associated data

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

Data Deletion Information

Data That Will Be Deleted:

  • Your account information (email and user ID)
  • All meal analysis history
  • All food images you've uploaded
  • Any user preferences or settings

Data Retention Period:

Upon deletion request, we immediately initiate the deletion process. All data will be completely removed from our systems within 30 days.

Data That May Be Retained:

  • Aggregate, anonymized analytics that don't identify individual users
  • Information required for legal compliance (if applicable)
  • Records of deletion requests for verification purposes
